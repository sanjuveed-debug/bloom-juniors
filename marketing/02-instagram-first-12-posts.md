### Post 2 (Wed) — FREE VALUE · carousel: 3 slides, big text
**Slide 1:** "It's *sss*… not *suh*." — The #1 phonics mistake parents make
**Slide 2:** When we add "uh" to sounds (suh-a-tuh), kids can't blend them into words. *sss-a-t* → sat ✅ *suh-a-tuh* → suhatuh ❌
**Slide 3:** Say the sound as short and clean as possible. That one habit makes blending click.
**Caption:**
Teachers call these "pure sounds" — and they're the reason some kids blend words easily while others get stuck.

Try it tonight: say *mmm*, *sss*, *fff* with your child — no "uh" at the end. That's it. That's the tip.

(Bloom Juniors uses pure sounds recorded for all 44 phonemes — free at bloomjuniors.com)
